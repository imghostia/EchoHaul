Safety Tips for Vehicle Emergencies

  • Stay calm and assess the situation. Check for injuries and call for medical assistance if needed.
  • Move to a safe location, if possible, and turn on hazard lights to alert other drivers.
  • Contact emergency services immediately by dialing 911. Provide accurate details about the location and situation.
  • If you have a first aid kit in your vehicle, use it to provide basic assistance while waiting for professional help.
  • Follow the guidance of law enforcement and emergency responders at the scene.
